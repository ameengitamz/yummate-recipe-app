A classic Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, and pancetta. Creamy, rich, and absolutely delicious! Perfect for a cozy dinner at home.

üõí Ingredients (4 servings)

400g spaghetti pasta
200g pancetta, diced
4 large eggs
100g Parmesan cheese, grated
2 cloves garlic, minced
Black pepper to taste
Salt to taste
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Quantities automatically adjust when you change servings above

üîß Instructions

1
Bring a large pot of salted water to boil for the pasta.
2
Cook pancetta in a large skillet until crispy and golden.
3
In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan, and black pepper.
4
Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.
5
Combine hot pasta with pancetta, then quickly mix in egg mixture off heat.
6
Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and fresh black pepper!
